Our Mission

We bring technology to you.

Our mission can be summarized into the 8 following tenets:


  1. To bring knowledge to the the community around us.
  2. To service the community.
  3. To create a positive, helpful and friendly tutorial enviornment.
  4. To attend all meetings and workshops on time to the best of our ability.
  5. To maintain a positive attitude when faced with problems.
  6. To remain diligent and aware of new technological improvements.
  7. To address all members of the society with respect and dignity.
  8. To care for the enviornment and its appliances around us.
